How numbers are calculated

  • Monthly due applies to schools marked as paying (trial/basic/pro billing) with a SaaS plan: price per student × student count in the ERP.
  • Trial and schools without a plan show ₹0 due until they convert.
  • Paid (month) is the sum of payments you record on Payments received with a payment date in {{ month_label }}.
  • YTD paid is Jan 1–last day of {{ month_label }} in {{ year }}.
