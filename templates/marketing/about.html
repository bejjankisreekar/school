The product

One workspace for your institution

Admins run operations from the web. Teachers handle attendance, exams, and homework. Students and parents see marks, fees, and announcements. What appears in the menu depends on role and plan—compare live on Pricing.

Key terms

Tenant (your school)

Your data lives in a dedicated school context—never mixed with other institutions. That isolation is what multi-tenancy means here.

Plan (Basic / Pro / Premium)

A subscription tier that bundles features—exams, payroll, transport, and more unlock as you move up. Student-based pricing still applies.

Feature

A switchable capability: timetable, fees, library, etc. Operators map features to plans so the product matches what you bought.

Roles

Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent—each sees tailored menus. Platform staff use a separate super-admin surface for support.

Plans

Basic, Pro, Premium

Same product code—different included modules. The pricing page always shows the current matrix.

Basic

Foundation

Students, faculty, classes, subjects, and academic year—the structural layer new digital programs need first.

Pro

Daily operations

Attendance, timetable, exams, homework, messaging, and broadcasts—fewer spreadsheets, more signal.

Premium

Full campus stack

Everything we publish: analytics, notifications, transport, hostel, library, branding—when you want the whole surface.

Dashboards and exports; optional AI-assisted summaries where enabled.

Branding & support

Logo and colors on supported plans; tracked support requests in-app.

What we ask when you enroll

The Enroll flow creates your tenant and admin login. Fields match how schools actually register.

Society / registered name

Required. Trust, society, or RWA legal name so records match registration papers.

Country code + mobile

Split fields for country and national number—fewer typos; we store a full international number.

Website & board

Optional site URL and affiliation (e.g. CBSE) to pre-fill your school profile.

Plan preference & trial

Choose Basic, Pro, or Premium intent (Premium pre-selected). Trial uses that footprint before billing.
