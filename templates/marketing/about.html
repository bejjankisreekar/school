Our Story

School ERP was born from a simple observation: educational institutions spend too much time on manual administrative tasks instead of what matters most — teaching and learning.

We built a team of educators and technologists to create a platform that brings together student data, fee management, attendance, exams, and communication into one unified system. Today, we serve schools and NGOs across regions, helping them digitize operations and deliver better outcomes.

We are committed to continuous improvement, listening to our customers, and building features that make school administration effortless.
